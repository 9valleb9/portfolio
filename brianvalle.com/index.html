About

I am Brian Valle

Entrepreneur and Leader

I have had a successful career in technology that spans over twenty years in video technology, leading teams in Operations, Software Development, and Data Science. Throughout my career, I have been fortunate to grow into positions that allow me to use my broad range of technology and life skills. While I have been primarily focused on video platforms transitioning from legacy Cable TV systems to what we all know and use today with IP Video, the roles I have performed translate well into many other business functions.

Additionally, I have been fortunate enough to start my own successful retail company, The Aid Station. The concept of the business was to be 'a running shoe store with a beer bar'. The target market was intended to serve the trail running community, but as the business grew the business pivoted to serve other customers as we identified areas of opportunity. Since day 1, the business was successful. I have moved on from the company, but it still thrives, and you can see it for yourself in downtown Auburn, CA or online by clicking on the link in my portfolio section.
